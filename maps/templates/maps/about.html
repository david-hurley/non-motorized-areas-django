This page was created out of a desire to more easily view non-motorized and motorized zones in the Sea to Sky. As someone who backcountry skis and snowmobiles I found it difficult to find high-resolution maps or downloadable formats for phone applications such as Gaia. This made it very challenging to know the exact location of the non-motorized boundaries. While resources did exist, such as iMapBC where recreation zones can be visualized and downloaded, none that I could find were easily accessible to the everyday person.

IMPORTANT: Presented winter recreation areas are in effect from December 1 to May 31 annually and refer to non-commercial activities.
